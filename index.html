Principal of Hair Transplantation

Hair transplantation surgery is based on the principal of ‘Donor Dominance’. Hair at he back and side continue to grow throughout the life of a person, even in a man with severe hair loss. These hair are resistant to the hormonal influence. When the hair is transplanted in bald area, it continues to grow naturally and can be washed, trimmed and styled as one wants to. This donar area will permit harvesting of 4000- 5000 or more grafts in most people planned in one or multiple sessions.
