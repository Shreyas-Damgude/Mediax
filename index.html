Principle of Hair Transplantation

Hair transplantation surgery is based on the principal of ‘Donor Dominance’. Hair at he back and side continue to grow throughout the life of a person, even in a man with severe hair loss. These hair are resistant to the hormonal influence. When the hair is transplanted in bald area, it continues to grow naturally and can be washed, trimmed and styled as one wants to. Frequently Asked Questions

- Although uncommon, hair transplant surgery may have temporary side effects and associated risks. These can encompass minor concerns such as slight bleeding, swelling, itching, discomfort, temporary numbness, and reduced sensation for a brief period. The majority of these side effects are temporary and typically resolve fully within the initial 10-30 days, given proper post-care, follow-up, and medication.
- Following a hair transplant, the newly transplanted hair grows in a manner similar to natural hair. Initially, there might be shedding of the transplanted hair within the first 3 months, after which new hair begins to grow from the transplanted follicles. This fresh hair continues to grow normally and can be styled, cut, and dyed just like your natural hair.
- Hair transplant procedures are typically done under local anesthesia, so you shouldn't feel pain during the surgery. Any discomfort following the procedure is usually minimal and can be eased with prescribed pain relief medication.
- The duration of a hair transplant procedure depends on the number of grafts being transplanted but generally takes anywhere from 4 to 8 hours. Larger sessions that involve a significant number of grafts may require a longer time or multiple sessions.
- The three main hair transplant techniques are FUE, FUT, and DHI. FUT Hair Transplant involves removing a strip from the donor area (back of the scalp) with hair follicles, while FUE Hair Transplant extracts individual follicles for transplantation. DHI Hair Transplant is a modified FUE method where grafts are implanted quickly for higher survival rates. DHI at Majestic Hair and Skin Clinic achieves a remarkable up to 98% graft survival rate. The best technique depends on factors like hair loss condition, donor area, personal preferences, and surgeon's advice. Each technique has its benefits and suitability for different cases.
